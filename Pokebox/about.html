FAQ

What is this website and who is it for?

Our website is a platform designed for collectors and fans of the Pokémon Trading Card Game. Here, you can buy, sell, and trade cards with other members of the community. The platform is built to make trading cards easy, convenient, and secure for everyone.

How can I buy a card?

To buy a card, simply log into your account, find the card you want using the search or filters, and add it to your cart. Once added, you can proceed to checkout and complete the purchase by choosing your preferred payment method.

How do I list a card for sale?

To sell a card, log into your account, go to the sell section, and provide all the necessary details about the card: its name, condition, and price. Once confirmed, your listing will be available for other users to see and buy.

How does card trading work?

Card trading is done through a special section where users can offer their cards and look for trades. If you see a card you like, you can send a trade request to the owner. Both parties can then agree on the terms and complete the exchange.

Can I trust the sellers on this website?

We strive to maintain high standards of security, so each user has a rating and review system. You can always check a seller’s profile, their rating, and read reviews from other buyers to make an informed decision before buying.

Are there any fees?

Yes, we charge a small fee on each sale to help maintain and improve the website. The exact fee amount will be displayed during the transaction process, so you’ll always know how much will be deducted from the sale.

What if I want to return a card?

Card returns are possible, but the terms depend on the individual seller. We recommend checking the seller’s return policy before purchasing. If there are any issues, you can always reach out to our support team for assistance.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us through the contact form on the website or by sending an email to our support address found in the "Contact" section. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

What features are planned for the future?

We are constantly improving our platform and plan to add new features like tournament participation, advanced card search filters, and reward systems for active users.
